Pricing Questions

Is there a setup fee?
No. There's no setup fee, no onboarding fee, and no design deposit. Your first invoice is your first month's subscription. If you request a free preview and don't move forward, you pay nothing.
Do I own the site?
You own your content — all the copy, images, and pages we create for your business are yours. If you cancel and want to take the files elsewhere, we provide them. We don't hold your site or content hostage.
What happens if I cancel?
You can cancel at any time with no penalty. Your site stays live through the end of the billing period. After that, we can transfer your files to you or to a hosting provider of your choice. There are no cancellation fees.
How long does it take to go live?
We deliver a live preview within 48 hours of receiving your intake form. Most clients request 1–2 minor changes, which we turn around same-day. Total time from intake to live site is typically 3–4 days.
Can I upgrade or downgrade later?
Yes. You can move between plans at any time. Upgrading is prorated for the remainder of the billing period. Downgrading takes effect at the next renewal. There are no fees for switching plans.
What does "SEO" actually include?
It depends on the plan. Starter includes on-page SEO — proper title tags, meta descriptions, header structure, and clean URL paths. Growth adds local SEO: Google Business Profile optimization, LocalBusiness and Service schema markup, and city-targeted pages. Authority adds weekly content, citation building, and monthly reporting on rankings and traffic.
