Why Oak Park homeowners call Summit Roofing

Oak Park is one of our specialty service areas because of the concentration of historic homes — many on the National Register, with strict architectural review requirements. We have specific experience with Oak Park’s historic preservation guidelines and can navigate the approval process for roof replacements that need to maintain period-appropriate materials and appearance.

Oak Park neighborhoods we serve

Frank Lloyd Wright Historic District, Ridgeland Historic District, Gunderson Historic District, Central Oak Park, South Oak Park. If you don’t see your neighborhood listed, call us — chances are we cover it.

What's specific about roofing in Oak Park

Historic district roofing

Many Oak Park homes are within historic districts that require approval for exterior changes, including roofing material color and profile. We handle the historic commission application for clients.

Material specialization

Cedar shake, slate, copper, and architectural asphalt shingles in heritage colors. Standing seam copper for premium homes.

Older roof complexity

Oak Park homes from the early 1900s often have multiple roof planes, dormers, turrets, and complex flashing geometry that takes significantly more skill than tract housing. Our crews are trained for it.

Drive distance

About 3.5 hours from Springfield. We schedule Chicago-area work in clusters and offer remote video estimates for initial scoping.
