Common Storm Damage Types

Know the signs. Even minor damage can lead to major problems if left unrepaired.

Hail Damage

Hail can crack, dent, and dislodge shingles, exposing your roof deck to water infiltration. Damage may not be visible from the ground.

  • Dents in shingles or metal
  • Granule loss on shingles
  • Cracked or split shingles
  • Dented gutters and downspouts

Wind Damage

High winds can lift, tear, and blow off shingles, especially at edges, ridges, and transitions. Even 50 mph gusts can cause significant damage.

  • Missing or lifted shingles
  • Exposed underlayment or deck
  • Damaged ridge caps
  • Debris on roof surface

Fallen Trees & Debris

Falling branches and trees can cause catastrophic roof damage requiring immediate structural assessment and emergency repairs.

  • Visible structural damage
  • Punctured or collapsed areas
  • Interior water intrusion
  • Sagging roofline
