About Summit Roofing

Family-owned since 2001. Built on integrity, craftsmanship, and a commitment to protecting Springfield homes.

Our Story

Summit Roofing Co. was founded in 2001 by Tom and Linda Mercer with a simple mission: deliver honest, quality roofing work at a fair price. What started as a two-person operation working out of a pickup truck has grown into one of Central Illinois' most respected roofing companies.

Today, Summit employs over 40 skilled professionals and has completed more than 3,000 residential and commercial roofing projects. Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of the values that built this company: integrity, craftsmanship, and treating every customer's home as if it were our own.

"We don't cut corners. We don't use subcontractors. And we stand behind every roof we install with the industry's strongest warranty."

— Tom Mercer, Founder & President
