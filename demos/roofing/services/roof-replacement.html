Roof Replacement in Springfield, IL

Full tear-off and replacement using GAF Timberline architectural shingles. Lifetime materials warranty. Workmanship guaranteed for 25 years. Most jobs completed in 1-2 days.

What's included in a Summit Roofing replacement

A full roof replacement isn't just shingles. Cutting corners on the underlayment, ventilation, or flashing is what separates a roof that lasts 25 years from one that fails in 12. Here's exactly what we install on every replacement, no matter the package you choose.

Full tear-off down to deck

We remove every layer of existing roofing material. No installing over old shingles. The deck is inspected and replaced where needed.

Synthetic underlayment

Premium synthetic underlayment installed across the entire roof deck. Felt paper is obsolete. Synthetic doesn't tear, doesn't absorb water, and lasts the life of the roof.

Ice and water shield

Self-adhering ice and water barrier installed at all roof edges, valleys, and penetrations. This is what prevents ice dam leaks during Illinois winters.

GAF Timberline HDZ shingles

Architectural shingles with the StainGuard Plus algae warranty and the LayerLock technology that holds nails better than any competitor in real wind tests.

New flashing & ventilation

All flashing around chimneys, vents, and walls replaced with new aluminum or copper. Proper attic ventilation calculated to your roof size.

Site cleanup & magnetic sweep

Magnetic sweep across your property and driveway to catch every nail. Yard left cleaner than when we arrived. Old materials hauled to certified disposal.

The lifetime warranty, in plain English

Most roofing warranties are written to be confusing. Ours is simple: GAF backs the materials for the entire life of the original roof, and we back our installation workmanship for 25 years. If something is covered and fails, we replace it — you do not pay anything out of pocket. Because we’re GAF Master Elite, we can extend that protection to the GAF Golden Pledge warranty, which is the strongest warranty in the residential roofing industry.

What's the GAF Golden Pledge?

It's a 50-year non-prorated warranty covering both materials and workmanship, transferable to a new homeowner. Less than 2% of roofing contractors in the country can offer it. Read the details ›

Roof replacement cost in Springfield

The average asphalt shingle roof replacement in Springfield runs between $9,500 and $18,500 depending on the square footage, roof pitch, and whether ventilation or decking work is needed. Larger homes or steep-pitch roofs run higher. Metal roofing and tile run significantly more. Every quote we provide is fixed and itemized — you see exactly what each line costs, with no commission-driven upsells.
