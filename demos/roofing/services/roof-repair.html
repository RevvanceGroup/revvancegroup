Roof Repair in Springfield, IL

Leak repair, missing shingles, damaged flashing, ice dam removal. Same-day service for active leaks. Permanent fixes only — no temporary patches that fail in six months.

Most common roof repairs we handle

About 70% of the calls we get are for repairs, not replacements. Most leaks come from the same handful of failure points, and most can be fixed for under $1,500 if caught early. Here’s what we see most often in Springfield homes.

Active leaks

Water in your ceiling, attic, or down a wall. We find the actual entry point (often not where the stain is), seal it, and document the repair.

Missing or damaged shingles

High wind, hail, or fallen tree limbs that took out a section. Color-matched replacement shingles installed with sealing strip and storm nails.

Chimney & vent flashing

Flashing pulls away from chimneys and vents over time. Counterflashing replacement, sealant restoration, and step-flashing repair where needed.

Ice dam damage

Spring repair after winter ice dams. Damaged shingles replaced, ice and water shield installed where missing, ventilation evaluated.

Pipe boot & vent stack repair

The rubber gaskets around plumbing vents crack after 8-12 years. Almost every roof over a decade old has at least one cracked boot.

Hail damage

Hail leaves dent marks that look minor but compromise the shingle granules. We document for insurance and replace what's damaged.

When repair makes sense — and when it doesn’t

Repair is the right call when damage is isolated, the rest of the roof is in good shape, and the roof has more than 5-7 years of useful life remaining. Once damage covers more than 30% of the roof or the system is past its expected service life, repair turns into throwing money away. We’ll tell you straight when replacement makes more economic sense, even though our repair work is profitable to us.

Active leak right now?

Repair pricing — what to expect

Most isolated repairs run $350 to $1,500. Pipe boot replacement is typically $250-$450. Flashing repair runs $500-$1,200 depending on the chimney or wall length. Section repairs after wind or hail are $800-$2,500 and may be covered by your homeowners insurance. We provide written quotes and never start work without your approval.
