Inflation Reduction Act

Federal Tax Credits Worth Thousands

The 2022 Inflation Reduction Act created the largest set of residential energy efficiency incentives in US history. If you're installing a qualifying high-efficiency HVAC system, you may be eligible for substantial federal tax credits and rebates.

  • Heat Pump Tax Credit — up to $2,000
    Section 25C of the IRS code. Covers 30% of installed cost up to $2,000 for qualifying ENERGY STAR heat pumps.
  • High-Efficiency Central AC — up to $600
    Splits systems meeting CEE Highest Efficiency Tier qualify for 30% of cost up to $600 per year.
  • Gas Furnace — up to $600
    ENERGY STAR certified gas furnaces at 97% AFUE or higher qualify for the same $600 credit.
  • HEEHRA Rebate — up to $8,000
    The Home Electrification & Appliance Rebate program provides direct point-of-sale rebates for heat pump installations for income-qualified households (below 150% of area median income).
  • Electrical Panel Upgrade — up to $600
    If a panel upgrade is required to support your new system, it may qualify independently.

Meridian handles the paperwork. We identify eligible equipment during your estimate, provide Manufacturer Certification Statements required for filing, and give you a pre-filled summary sheet to hand your tax preparer. For HEEHRA rebates, we coordinate directly with the state administrator.

Financing FAQ

Common Questions, Clear Answers

What is the minimum credit score to qualify for HVAC financing?+

Most Meridian financing partners require a minimum FICO score of 600 for basic approval and 660+ for the best promotional rates including 0% APR offers. Wells Fargo and Synchrony both evaluate multiple factors beyond credit score, including income, debt-to-income ratio, and payment history. Buyers with scores below 600 may still qualify through secondary lenders at higher rates, and we offer a 90-day same-as-cash in-house option for urgent repair needs.

Does applying hurt my credit score?+

The initial pre-qualification uses a soft credit inquiry, which does not affect your credit score. You can see your estimated rate and approval odds without any impact. Only when you accept an offer and proceed with the full application does the lender perform a hard inquiry, which can reduce your score by a few points temporarily — typically recovering within a few months of on-time payments. Meridian always runs pre-qualification first.

How soon can I install after approval?+

Once financing is approved, Meridian typically schedules installation within 2 to 5 business days. Straight replacements of existing equipment (same tonnage, same configuration) complete in a single 6-8 hour day. Larger projects with ductwork modifications or fuel source changes (gas to electric heat pump) run 1.5–2 days. We work around your schedule, including early mornings and Saturday installs when needed.

Can I pay off early without a penalty?+

Yes. All financing plans we offer through Wells Fargo and Synchrony have zero prepayment penalties. You can pay off the full balance at any time with no extra fees. On promotional 0% APR plans, paying the balance before the promotional period ends locks in zero interest charges. On fixed-rate long-term plans, early payoff saves you the remaining scheduled interest.

What happens if I'm declined?+

If the primary lender declines, we have secondary options including payment plans through local credit unions, lease-to-own programs, and a no-credit-check 90-day same-as-cash option for repairs under $3,000. We also work with buyers to improve qualification by adding a co-signer, documenting income more thoroughly, or making a larger down payment. We rarely walk away from a customer over credit alone.

Is the interest tax deductible?+

For primary residences, HVAC financing interest is generally not tax deductible unless the loan is secured through a home equity line of credit (HELOC) or home equity loan. However, the HVAC equipment itself may qualify for significant federal tax credits under the Inflation Reduction Act — up to $2,000 for heat pumps and up to $600 for high-efficiency AC systems. Consult a tax professional for your specific situation.

Can I finance repairs, or only installations?+

Yes, we finance repairs too. Meridian offers financing on repairs over $500 through the same partner network. For repairs between $500 and $3,000, we also offer an in-house 90-day same-as-cash option with no credit check — helpful for unexpected compressor failures, heat exchanger replacements, or major refrigerant leak repairs. This keeps families out of the emergency credit card trap.

How does 0% APR really work?+

0% APR promotional financing means you pay no interest for a set period — typically 12, 18, or 24 months. You make fixed monthly payments during that window. If the full balance is paid before the promotional period ends, no interest is ever charged. If a balance remains at the end, the full deferred interest calculated from day one may be applied retroactively. Stay on schedule and it's genuinely free money to spread out.
