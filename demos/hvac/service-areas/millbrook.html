Serving Millbrook Homes Since 2013

Millbrook was mostly pasture and scattered acreage properties when Meridian started taking calls out that way in 2013. The Preserves at Millbrook opened up in 2015, Stonebridge followed a couple of years later, and by 2019 we had a standing Wednesday and Friday route through town. Today, Millbrook is one of our fastest-growing service areas.

What makes Millbrook different from Cedar Grove or Springfield is lot size. A lot of Millbrook homes sit on half-acre to two-acre lots, which changes the HVAC picture in a few specific ways: longer refrigerant line sets (more chance for leak points), bigger footprints with more solar gain on west-facing walls, and more homes that use geothermal or multi-zone systems to keep conditioning cost under control.

The climate is identical to our home base — triple-digit summers, humid Gulf air, mild winters, occasional multi-day freeze events. So the sizing rules of thumb and the heat-pump case still apply. The work just takes a little more time on the larger properties.
