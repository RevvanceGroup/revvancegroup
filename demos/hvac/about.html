Who We Are

17 Years. 12,400 Installs. One Reputation.

We're the HVAC company your neighbor keeps telling you about. Locally owned, NATE-certified, and built on a simple promise: honest diagnostics, fair prices, work that gets done right the first time.

Our Story

Founded on a Broken Promise

In 2008, Ray Vasquez was working as a commission-paid installer for one of the big HVAC chains in Texas. He watched technicians get pressured to upsell $10,000 systems to homeowners who needed a $300 capacitor. He watched senior techs lose their jobs for "not hitting sales quotas."

He quit, took out a second mortgage, bought one used service truck, and started Meridian Heating & Air with a radical idea: pay technicians a straight salary. Let them do their jobs. Fix problems, don't create them.

Seventeen years later, Meridian runs 14 trucks out of Cedar Grove, employs 22 NATE-certified technicians, and has completed over 12,400 installations. Ray still works on jobs every week. He's still not paying anyone commission.

62% of our business comes from customer referrals. That's the only metric we care about.

What We Stand For

Our Operating Principles

Not marketing copy. These are actual rules our team follows on every single job.

Fix First, Replace Last

Our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair before recommending replacement. We only replace when math and age genuinely call for it.

Flat-Rate, Upfront Pricing

You see the exact price before any work starts. No hourly billing surprises, no "by the way, we also found..." mid-job upsells.

Straight-Salary Technicians

Nobody on our team earns commission. Your technician has zero financial incentive to recommend anything beyond what you actually need.

Respect Your Time

Real arrival windows — not "sometime between 8 and 5." We text you the tech's photo and live GPS on the way.

Do It Right, Not Fast

We install to manufacturer spec, not to whatever's quickest. Commissioning reports, static pressure readings, refrigerant charge verified — documented on every job.

Own Our Mistakes

If we install something wrong, we come back and fix it — on our dime, no questions. Every job backed by a written 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Credentials

Licensed, Certified, Accredited

Every technician on our team carries NATE certification — the HVAC industry's highest credential. The company carries every license and insurance type Texas requires.

NATE Certified

North American Technician Excellence. The HVAC industry's premier certification — requires passing rigorous technical exams in heating, cooling, heat pumps, and air distribution. Only about 30% of working HVAC technicians hold it. Every Meridian field technician does.

EPA Section 608

Universal Certification. Required by federal law for anyone handling refrigerants. Our team holds Type I, II, and III Universal certifications — the highest level.

Texas TACLA Licensed

TACLA #B024891. Our Class A Air Conditioning and Refrigeration Contractor license, issued by the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation. Verifiable online at tdlr.texas.gov.

BBB A+ Accredited

Since 2010. Better Business Bureau A+ rating with zero unresolved complaints in our history. Accredited member of the Central Texas BBB.

Carrier Factory Authorized

Top 5% of dealers. Carrier's highest dealer tier, requiring ongoing training, minimum installation volume, warranty response standards, and customer satisfaction benchmarks.

Fully Bonded & Insured

$2M general liability. Workers' comp for every employee. Bonded for every job. We'll provide a certificate of insurance on request before any work begins.
