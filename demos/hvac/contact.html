Quick Questions

Contacting Meridian — FAQ

How fast will someone call me back after I submit the form?+

During business hours (Mon–Fri 7 AM–7 PM, Sat 8 AM–5 PM) you'll get a call back within 15 minutes. Forms submitted after hours are returned by 8 AM the next business day. True emergencies — no cool air in triple-digit heat, no heat in a freeze, refrigerant leaks, gas smells — should call (512) 555-0143 directly for 24/7 dispatch.

Is there someone actually answering the phone at 2 AM?+

Yes. A real Meridian dispatcher — not an answering service, not a call center in another state. Our after-hours line routes to our on-call dispatcher's cell phone. They can confirm your address, dispatch a truck, and give you an arrival window while you're still on the call.

Can I just walk into your office without an appointment?+

Absolutely. We're at 2847 Commerce Drive in Cedar Grove, open Mon–Fri 7 AM–7 PM and Saturday 8 AM–5 PM. Our front office has a waiting area, and we're happy to answer quick questions, show you equipment we install, or schedule you on the spot. Coffee is free.

Do you charge for estimates?+

Estimates for new system installation or replacement are always 100% free, including an in-home assessment, load calculation, and written quote. Diagnostic service calls on existing equipment carry a $79 trip fee, which is waived if you proceed with any repair we recommend.

What if I only need a question answered — no service?+

Call or email us anytime. We answer general HVAC questions for free — no obligation, no pitch. We genuinely believe an informed homeowner is a better customer long-term. If we can walk you through a $20 filter change instead of selling you a service call, we will.

Prefer to Just Pick Up the Phone?

We get it. Call us — a real Meridian team member will answer in under 30 seconds.
