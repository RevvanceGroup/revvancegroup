Choosing the Right Brand

Honest Brand Comparison

The honest answer is that the brand matters less than the installation. A perfectly-installed Goodman lasts longer than a poorly-installed Trane. That said, here’s how we typically guide brand choice for Cedar Grove customers:

If you’re cost-conscious but want quality

Rheem Pro Partner. Mid-tier pricing with 10-year warranty when properly registered. Good service network. Reliable parts availability.

If you want the best central system available

Carrier Infinity or Trane XV20i. Variable-speed compressors, premium controls, longest service life when properly maintained. Premium pricing.

If you want the most efficient system money can buy

Lennox SL28XCV at 28 SEER2. Highest efficiency rating available in residential central HVAC. Premium pricing.

If you’re going ductless

Mitsubishi Hyper-Heat for cold-climate performance, Daikin Fit for slim profile and great efficiency. Both excellent.

If you’re replacing a furnace and AC together

Match the brand to the existing communicating-system infrastructure if any. New installs default to your budget tier within Carrier, Trane, or Rheem.
