Cat6 Ethernet Pre-Wire

Hardwired Cat6 to every room beats Wi-Fi for streaming, gaming, work-from-home, and smart device reliability. Best done during construction or remodel, but retrofit is doable in most homes.

Structured Wiring Panel

Smart Switch Retrofits

WAP & Mesh Network Wiring

Smart Doorbell & Camera Power

Hardwired doorbells (Ring Pro, Nest Hello) require proper transformer and chime wiring. PoE security cameras need ethernet runs to each location with surge protection.

Whole-Home Audio Speaker Wiring

In-ceiling and in-wall speakers (Sonos, Klipsch, Polk) wired back to a central amplifier rack. Done right during construction; retrofit possible in most homes.

Why Hardwire Beats Wi-Fi for Smart Homes

A modern smart home easily has 50+ connected devices: thermostats, security cameras, doorbells, smart switches, plugs, locks, sensors, displays, and entertainment devices. When all of those try to compete for Wi-Fi bandwidth, you get the dropouts and lag everyone hates.

Hardwiring solves this. Cameras, hubs, displays, gaming consoles, and TVs run on ethernet, freeing Wi-Fi for the truly mobile devices. The setup is more reliable, faster, and more secure (ethernet traffic doesn’t broadcast).

For new construction or major remodels, this is straightforward. For existing homes, we run cabling through attics, basements, and wall channels — the level of difficulty depends on house construction. Free assessment to identify what’s feasible.
