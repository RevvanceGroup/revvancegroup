Austin FAQ

Common Questions from Austin Homeowners

Do you serve my neighborhood in Austin?
Yes. We service all Austin zip codes from 78701 to 78799. Whether you're in Hyde Park, South Congress, East Austin, Westlake, or the Domain area, we dispatch from our South Austin location and can typically reach you the same day for non-emergency work.
Do you handle permits with the City of Austin?
Yes, completely. We submit all permit applications to Austin's Development Services Department, schedule the city inspection, and are on-site when the inspector arrives. You don't need to deal with the permitting office at any point.
Do you work with Austin Energy for EV charger rebates?
Yes. Austin Energy offers rebates for qualifying Level 2 EV charger installations. We're familiar with the current program requirements and file the rebate paperwork as part of the installation at no extra charge. The rebate is paid directly to you by Austin Energy after the work is inspected.
How quickly can you respond to an electrical emergency in Austin?
We offer 24/7 emergency service throughout Austin. Because we're based in the city, dispatch times are typically shorter than for contractors coming from surrounding areas. For true emergencies involving sparking, burning smells, or complete power loss, call (512) 847-4200 directly — don't use the website form.
