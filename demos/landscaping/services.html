What We Do

Our Service Categories

From design to ongoing care, we handle every aspect of your outdoor environment.

Landscape Design & Installation

Our certified designers create custom outdoor plans that reflect your style and work with your property's unique conditions. From concept sketches to 3D renderings, you'll see your new landscape before a single plant goes in the ground.

  • Custom design with 3D visualization
  • Native and adaptive plant selection
  • Grading and drainage solutions
  • Outdoor lighting design
  • Complete project management

Lawn Care & Maintenance

A healthy lawn is the foundation of a great landscape. Our maintenance programs combine the science of turf management with the art of meticulous care to keep your grass thick, green, and weed-free.

  • Weekly mowing with sharp-blade cutting
  • Edging, trimming, and blowing
  • Seasonal fertilization programs
  • Weed control and pre-emergent treatment
  • Aeration and overseeding

Hardscaping & Patios

Extend your living space outdoors with beautifully crafted patios, walkways, retaining walls, and fire features. We use premium natural stone, pavers, and materials designed to withstand the elements and age gracefully.

  • Bluestone, flagstone, and paver patios
  • Retaining walls and terracing
  • Walkways and stepping stone paths
  • Outdoor kitchens and fire pits
  • Seat walls and pergolas
Plan Your Patio →

Tree & Shrub Care

Healthy trees and shrubs add structure, shade, and value to your property. Our ISA Certified Arborist provides expert care from planting through maturity, ensuring your woody plants thrive for generations.

  • Professional pruning and shaping
  • Tree health assessments
  • Disease and pest management
  • New tree and shrub planting
  • Storm damage cleanup
Schedule Tree Care →

Irrigation Systems

Smart watering saves water, saves money, and keeps your landscape consistently hydrated. We design, install, and maintain efficient irrigation systems with modern smart controllers and rain sensors.

  • Custom system design and installation
  • Smart controller programming
  • Rain and soil moisture sensors
  • Drip irrigation for garden beds
  • Seasonal startup and winterization
Get an Irrigation Quote →

Seasonal Cleanup

Every season brings new needs for your yard. Our seasonal cleanup services prepare your property for what's coming and clean up what's passed, keeping your landscape looking its best year-round.

  • Spring cleanup and bed preparation
  • Fall leaf removal and bed winterizing
  • Mulch installation and refresh
  • Seasonal annual plantings
  • Snow damage assessment and repair
