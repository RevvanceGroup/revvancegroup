About GreenScape

Our passion for outdoor spaces started with a single garden. Today we're transforming entire communities.

Our Story

Built on Passion, Grown with Purpose

GreenScape Landscaping was founded in 2011 by Marcus Rivera, a lifelong gardener who saw too many homes with uninspired yards and too many landscaping companies delivering cookie-cutter results.

What started as a one-truck operation doing weekend lawn care has grown into a full-service landscape design and maintenance company serving over 500 properties across northern New Jersey.

Our philosophy is simple: every outdoor space has potential, and every homeowner deserves a yard that brings them joy. We combine horticultural science with artistic design to create landscapes that are as healthy as they are beautiful.

Today, our team of 18 professionals includes certified arborists, landscape designers, and maintenance specialists who share a common passion for creating outdoor spaces that make people stop and stare.

Our Commitment

Sustainability at Our Core

We believe beautiful landscapes and environmental responsibility go hand in hand.

Native Plants

We prioritize native species that support local ecosystems, require less water, and naturally resist pests and disease.

Organic Options

100% organic fertilization and pest management programs available for every property we maintain.

Water Conservation

Smart irrigation with rain sensors, drip systems, and drought-tolerant plantings reduce water usage by up to 40%.

Eco-Friendly Practices

Electric equipment where possible, responsible waste disposal, and locally sourced materials for every project.

How We Work

Our Design Process

From the first handshake to the final walkthrough, here's what working with GreenScape looks like.

1

Free Consultation

We visit your property, listen to your vision, and discuss your goals, budget, and timeline. This no-obligation meeting helps us understand exactly what you're looking for and allows you to get a feel for how we work. We'll take photos, measurements, and notes.

2

Site Analysis

Our team conducts a thorough analysis of your property including soil testing, sun/shade mapping, drainage patterns, and assessment of existing plants and structures. This scientific foundation ensures every design decision is grounded in what will actually thrive in your specific conditions.

3

Custom Design

Our designers create detailed plans featuring plant selections, material choices, and layout specifications. For larger projects, we provide 3D renderings so you can visualize the final result. We refine the design together until it's exactly right.

4

Expert Installation

Our trained crew brings your design to life with precision and care. We protect your existing property, communicate daily progress, and ensure every plant, stone, and feature is placed exactly as designed. Most residential projects complete within 1-4 weeks.

5

Ongoing Maintenance

A beautiful landscape needs ongoing care to stay beautiful. We offer flexible maintenance packages to keep your investment looking its best season after season. From weekly lawn care to comprehensive estate management, we've got you covered.
