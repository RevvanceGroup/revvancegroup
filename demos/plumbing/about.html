About Elite Plumbing & HVAC

Over 20 years of trusted service, built on integrity, expertise, and a commitment to doing things right.

Our Story

Built on Honest Work Since 2004

Elite Plumbing & HVAC was founded in 2004 by Michael Donovan, a master plumber with a simple belief: homeowners deserve honest, quality work at a fair price. After spending 12 years working for large companies that prioritized volume over craftsmanship, Michael struck out on his own with one truck and a commitment to doing things differently.

What started as a one-person operation has grown into a team of 18 licensed technicians serving Springfield and 12 surrounding communities. Despite our growth, the values that built this company remain unchanged: transparent pricing, thorough work, and treating every customer's home as if it were our own.

Today, we handle everything from emergency pipe repairs to full HVAC installations, and we are proud that more than 60% of our business comes from repeat customers and referrals. That is the kind of trust you earn, not the kind you can buy.
