Plan Your Visit

Parking

Complimentary valet parking is available during dinner service (Tuesday through Saturday). A public parking garage is located one block east on Market Street, and metered street parking is available on Harvest Lane and surrounding streets.

Dress Code

Smart casual. We want you to feel comfortable while enjoying an elegant dining experience. Jackets are welcome but not required.

Accessibility

Our restaurant is fully wheelchair accessible, including restrooms and our private dining room. Please let us know of any special accommodations when making your reservation.

Dietary Accommodations

We happily accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary needs. Please inform us when booking or upon arrival so Chef Maria can prepare something special for you.
