Our Story

A Kitchen Built on Relationships

When Chef Maria Castellano returned from a decade in Italy's finest kitchens, she had one vision: a restaurant where every ingredient tells a story, and every dish is a conversation between farmer and chef.

Together with co-founder James Whitfield, she opened The Rustic Table in 2018, building relationships with local farms within 50 miles. The result is a menu that changes with the seasons and honors the land that feeds us.
