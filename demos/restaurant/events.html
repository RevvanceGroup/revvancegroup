Celebrations & Gatherings

Private Events

Your Event, Our Passion

Create Unforgettable Moments

From intimate celebrations to corporate gatherings, our team crafts bespoke dining experiences tailored to your vision. Let us handle the details while you savor the moment.

Private Dining Room

Up to 40 guests Custom menus

Our private dining room is an intimate space separated from the main restaurant, featuring its own entrance, dedicated service team, and customizable ambiance. Whether it is a rehearsal dinner, birthday celebration, or corporate event, we create a bespoke experience for every occasion.

  • Custom prix fixe or family-style menus
  • Full A/V equipment with screen and speakers
  • Dedicated event coordinator
  • Wine pairing available
  • Floral and decor coordination

Chef's Table Experience

8-12 guests 8 courses

An exclusive seat at the heart of our kitchen. Watch Chef Maria and her team create an 8-course tasting menu before your eyes, each dish paired with wines selected by our sommelier. A truly immersive culinary journey reserved for those who seek something extraordinary.

  • 8-course seasonal tasting menu
  • Wine or cocktail pairing included
  • Interactive kitchen-side seating
  • Personal introduction by Chef Maria
  • Take-home recipe card keepsake

Off-Site Catering

20-200 guests Full service

Bring The Rustic Table experience to your venue. Our off-site catering team delivers the same quality, creativity, and farm-to-table philosophy to weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations at the location of your choice.

  • Plated, family-style, or buffet service
  • Custom menu consultation with Chef Maria
  • Full bar and cocktail service available
  • Professional service staff included
  • Tableware and linen rentals available

Monthly Series

Wine Dinner Series

Join us the first Thursday of each month for an intimate evening of wine and cuisine. Each dinner features a guest winemaker and a custom 5-course menu designed to complement their selections.

May
Wines of Tuscany

A journey through Chianti, Brunello, and Super Tuscans paired with Italian-inspired farm dishes.

June
Summer Rosé

Light, refreshing rosés from Provence and the Willamette Valley with seasonal summer plates.

July
New World Reds

Bold wines from Napa, Mendoza, and Barossa Valley paired with our grilled mains.
