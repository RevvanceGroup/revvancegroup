From First Contact to Live Site in 48 Hours

Four steps. No discovery marathons, no content questionnaires, no waiting weeks for a template you could have found yourself. We build, you approve, it goes live.

01

Intake Day 0

Fill out a short form. We ask for your trade, your city and service area, the services you offer, and any competitors whose rankings you want to take. That's enough for us to get started.

No discovery call required — though if you want one, we're available. Most clients just fill out the form and let us work. We read what you send carefully. If something is unclear, we'll ask a quick follow-up by email rather than scheduling a meeting to ask it.

You don't need to send us photos, write descriptions of your services, or provide any copy. That's our job. We'll pull your service area, research the search landscape in your market, look at what your competitors are ranking for, and use that to structure your site.

What you provide: Trade, location, services, phone number, and any specific requests (e.g., "I want a financing page" or "I need to rank in these three suburbs").
02

Build Days 1–2

We build your site from scratch. That means designing the layout, writing all the copy, structuring the service pages, setting up city pages for your service area, implementing schema markup, configuring hosting, and wiring everything together.

The copy is written for your specific trade and your specific market — not repurposed from a template. A plumber in Phoenix gets different content than a plumber in Boston: different seasonal hooks, different competitor landscape, different search terms.

Schema markup is implemented from the start: LocalBusiness, Service pages, BreadcrumbList, and FAQPage where applicable. This isn't something we bolt on later — it's part of the build.

By the end of Day 2, you have a complete, hosted, functional website sitting at a preview URL. Not a mockup. Not a PDF. A live site you can click through on your phone.

What we deliver: A live preview URL with your complete site — design, copy, structure, schema, and mobile layout all in place.
03

Review Day 3

We send you the preview link and ask for your feedback. Most clients request 1–2 small changes — a phone number correction, a service we missed, a color preference. We turn those around the same day.

If you want more substantive changes — different layout, additional pages, a different direction on the copy — that's fine too. We'll work through it until you're satisfied. There's no limit on revision rounds before launch.

We'll also walk you through what's been built if you want a quick call — what pages exist, how the city targeting works, where the schema is implemented. Most clients don't need this, but it's available.

Typical revision requests: Phone number, hours, one missed service, a city we should add. Rarely anything structural.
04

Launch Days 3–4

Once you approve the site, we handle everything for launch. If you have an existing domain, we'll send you the DNS records to update — takes about five minutes on your registrar. If you need a domain, we can register one for you.

We configure your hosting, connect SSL, submit your sitemap to Google Search Console, and verify Google Business Profile connection where applicable. By the end of Day 4, your site is live at your domain, indexed by Google, and ready to rank.

Ongoing from there: hosting, maintenance, and your monthly SEO work continue automatically. You don't have to manage anything. Updates happen on your schedule — request a change by email and we turn it around within one business day.

What you do at launch: Update your DNS records (5 minutes). That's it. We handle everything else.

What You Don't Have to Do

Working with most agencies involves a long list of tasks that get handed back to you. With Revvance Group, that list is short:

  • You don't write copy. We write all the content — service descriptions, city pages, about sections, FAQs. You can edit anything we write, but you're not starting from a blank page.
  • You don't pick templates. We don't send you a library of 40 themes to choose from. We design your site based on what converts for your trade and market.
  • You don't deal with hosting. Hosting, SSL, uptime monitoring, backups — all handled. You never get a GoDaddy invoice or a WordPress update notification.
  • You don't manage SEO tools. We run Search Console, track rankings, submit sitemaps, and handle citation building. You get a monthly report on Authority plans; on other plans, you can ask us for a status update anytime.
  • You don't wait weeks for a draft. The preview is ready in 48 hours. If you've worked with a web agency before, this is the part that usually surprises people.

Process Questions

What if I don't have a domain yet?
We can register one for you, or help you choose one if you're unsure. Domain registration is included in the setup — just let us know in your intake form that you need a domain and we'll handle it. We'll also advise on naming if you have options to consider.
What if I want to make changes after launch?
All plans include at least one content update per month. Just email us what you want changed and we'll turn it around within one business day. On the Authority plan, updates are unlimited. There are no extra charges for changes — it's part of your monthly subscription.
Do you need access to my old site?
No. We build from scratch and don't need credentials for your current site. If there's specific content from your old site you want us to carry over — service descriptions, a bio, specific photos — just include it in your intake form or send it to us separately. We'll work it in.
What if I already have a site I want to keep?
We can build a new site on a staging URL while your current site stays live. Once you approve the new version, we switch the domain over. There's no downtime for your existing site during the build process. If you're not sure whether to replace your current site, the free preview is a good way to compare them side by side before you decide.

Fill out the intake form and we'll have a preview ready for you in 48 hours. No payment until you approve the site.
