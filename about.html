We Build Websites for the Trades

A contractor-only web agency. We don't take on retail brands, restaurants, or SaaS startups. Just plumbers, HVAC companies, roofers, electricians, and landscapers — and we've built the whole operation around that.

Who We Are

Revvance Group is a small, focused agency. We work exclusively with home service contractors — plumbers, HVAC companies, roofers, electricians, and landscapers. That's it. We made that decision early and we've stuck to it.

We're not a generalist agency that happens to take contractor clients. Every template we've built, every schema type we've implemented, every city page structure we use was designed around the specific way trade businesses generate leads. That specificity is the whole product.

Most web agencies will build a site for a dentist, a boutique hotel, a fintech startup, and a plumber — all in the same week, using the same general-purpose process. The work that comes out of that is functional but generic. It doesn't account for emergency call CTAs, seasonal service shifts, service radius targeting, or the trust signals that actually convert a homeowner searching for an electrician at 9pm.

We account for all of that, because it's all we do.

Why Contractor-Focused Design Is Different

A plumber's website has different conversion goals than a law firm's website. The user is often in a high-urgency situation — a burst pipe, no heat in January, a roof leak after a storm. The site needs to present a phone number and a clear call to action within seconds of landing. It needs to establish trust fast: license number, insurance status, years in business, recognizable service area.

Beyond the landing experience, contractor sites need a specific SEO architecture. A plumber in Denver who wants to rank for searches in Aurora, Lakewood, and Centennial needs individual city service pages — not a single "we serve the greater Denver area" line buried in the footer. Those pages need to be substantive, accurately reflect the services offered in each location, and be built with proper schema markup so Google understands the geographic relationship.

We build all of this into every site from the start. It's not an add-on. It's the baseline.

How We Work

The model is straightforward. You pay one flat monthly rate. That covers your site design, hosting, ongoing maintenance, and SEO work — no separate invoices for each. No hourly billing when you want to change your phone number. No project quotes when you want to add a page.

When you sign up, we build your site from scratch based on a short intake form. We research your market, write the copy, handle the technical setup, and deliver a live preview within 48 hours. You review it, request changes, and we turn those around the same day. Then it goes live.

You own your content. If you ever leave, the content is yours. We don't hold sites hostage or lock you into proprietary platforms that only we can edit. Everything is built on clean, portable HTML and CSS.

Month to month after that — no contracts. If the site isn't working for you, you can leave. We'd rather earn your business each month than lock you in.

What We Believe

Specificity over genericism

Generic websites produce generic results. Every contractor site we build is specific to a trade, a market, and a set of services. The copy is specific. The schema is specific. The city targeting is specific. That's what separates a site that ranks from one that doesn't.

Long-term relationships

SEO compounds over time. A site that's been building authority for 18 months is harder to displace than one that launched last month. We're set up for long relationships — monthly updates, ongoing content, regular schema refinement. The goal is a site that keeps improving.

Honest pricing

One flat rate. No setup fee, no project quotes, no add-on charges for things that should be included. You know what you're paying before you sign up. You know what's included. No surprises on your invoice.
