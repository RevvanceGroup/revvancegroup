Straight Talk

Questions We Get From Contractors Every Day

"I already have a website but it doesn't do anything."

Most contractor websites were built to look nice, not generate leads. We build sites with one purpose: make your phone ring. Click-to-call buttons, local SEO, city pages, trust signals — every element is there to convert a visitor into a paying customer. The demo we send you will show the difference within 60 seconds of clicking it.

"What does it cost?"

It depends on your trade, your service area, and how many pages your market needs to dominate. We don't list prices because the right answer for a one-truck plumber in a small town is different from a five-truck HVAC operation in a metro. We'll quote you on the demo call once we know what we're building.

"I'm not tech-savvy at all."

Good — you don't need to be. We handle 100% of the technical work. You never log in, never update anything, never deal with hosting. Your only job is answering the phone when new customers call from your site.

"How long until I see results?"

Your site goes live within 48 hours of approval. Calls can start immediately from direct traffic, paid ads, or your Google Business profile. Organic SEO rankings build over the following weeks and months as Google indexes and trusts your pages — there's no honest way to promise an exact timeline because every market is different.

"Why no contract?"

Because if we're doing our job, you won't want to leave. Long contracts exist to protect agencies that don't deliver. We'd rather earn your business every month than trap you in one.

"What if I want to leave and take my site?"

You can. The site is yours. We'll export the full codebase or migrate it to your hosting at any time, no fee, no hassle. We don't believe in holding contractor websites hostage.
