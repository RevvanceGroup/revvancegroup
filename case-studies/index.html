Our Work: Contractor Websites Across 3 Verticals

These aren't mockups or screenshots. Each is a fully-built, live website with real SEO structure, schema markup, mobile design, and the page architecture we use for actual clients. Click through them the same way a homeowner would.

Every demo was built to demonstrate what a real client site looks like on that plan tier — the page count, the content depth, the schema implementation, the city targeting approach. The business names are fictional, but the structure is identical to what we ship to paying clients.

When we build a free preview for a new client, it follows the same process as these demos: we research the market, write trade-specific copy, implement schema from the start, and deliver a clickable site within 48 hours. These demos give you a concrete picture of what that deliverable looks like across different trades and plan sizes.

HVAC

Meridian Heating & Air

Authority Plan demo

A full-scale HVAC demo built to show what an Authority plan looks like for a heating and cooling contractor. Nine service pages: AC repair, AC installation, furnace repair, heat pumps, mini-splits, indoor air quality, ductwork, commercial HVAC, and maintenance plans. Three city pages with market-specific content. A blog section, emergency service page, financing, brands page, and IRA tax credit/rebates page.

Schema implemented: HVACBusiness, Service (per service page), FAQPage, BreadcrumbList on every page. The emergency service page uses urgency-appropriate copy and a persistent click-to-call CTA that stays visible on scroll — a pattern specific to HVAC businesses that field after-hours calls.

  • 9 service detail pages
  • 3 city/area pages
  • Brands & rebates pages
  • Emergency service page
  • Financing + 5 blog posts
  • Full schema implementation

Authority Plan demo

The most comprehensive demo in the portfolio. Thirteen service pages covering panel upgrades, whole-home rewiring, EV charger installation, generator installation, lighting, outlets and switches, smart home wiring, surge protection, smoke detectors, code corrections (Federal Pacific/Zinsco panel danger lead magnet), commercial electrical, and emergency service. Six city pages with market-specific content. FAQ, financing, reviews, and a full blog.

Review integration shows a pattern for pulling in and displaying Google reviews with structured markup.
