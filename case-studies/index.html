Our Work: Contractor Websites Across 5 Verticals

These aren't mockups or screenshots. Each demo is a fully-built, live website with real SEO structure, schema markup, mobile design, and the page architecture we use for actual clients. Click through them the same way a homeowner would.

Every demo was built to demonstrate what a real client site looks like on that plan tier — the page count, the content depth, the schema implementation, the city targeting approach. The business names are fictional, but the structure is identical to what we ship to paying clients.

When we build a free preview for a new client, it follows the same process as these demos: we research the market, write trade-specific copy, implement schema from the start, and deliver a clickable site within 48 hours. These demos give you a concrete picture of what that deliverable looks like across different trades and plan sizes.

22 pages
HVAC

Meridian Heating & Air

Authority Plan demo

A full-scale HVAC demo built to show what an Authority plan looks like for a heating and cooling contractor. Six service pages covering AC repair, AC installation, heating repair, furnace replacement, heat pumps, and maintenance plans. Three city pages with individual service area content. A blog section, emergency HVAC service page, and financing options page.

Schema implemented: LocalBusiness, HVACBusiness, Service (per service page), FAQPage, BreadcrumbList. The emergency service page uses urgency-appropriate copy and a persistent click-to-call CTA that stays visible on scroll — a pattern specific to HVAC businesses that field after-hours calls.

  • 6 service pages
  • 3 city/area pages
  • Emergency service page
  • Financing page
  • Blog with category structure
  • Full schema implementation

Electrician

Voltage Electric Co.

Authority Plan demo

The most comprehensive demo in the portfolio. Eight service pages covering panel upgrades, whole-home rewiring, EV charger installation, generator installation, lighting, outlets and switches, commercial electrical, and emergency service. Six city pages with market-specific content. FAQ, financing, reviews, and a full blog.

Review integration shows a pattern for pulling in and displaying Google reviews with structured markup.

Plumbing

Clear Flow Plumbing

Starter Plan demo

A five-page plumbing demo built to show what a well-executed Starter plan looks like. Home, Services, About, Contact, and one city service page. The focus here is on conversion: a prominent phone number in the header, a fast-loading layout, license and insurance trust signals above the fold, and a simple service list that covers the core offerings homeowners search for.

This is the structure for a plumber who needs a professional presence online and wants to start ranking for their city before investing in a larger site. It's designed to be expanded — the service and city page structure is set up to accept additional pages as the business grows into a Growth or Authority plan.

  • Home, Services, About, Contact
  • 1 city service page
  • LocalBusiness schema
  • Click-to-call header CTA

5 pages
Roofing

Summit Roofing

Starter Plan demo

A five-page roofing demo structured around the specific trust signals that matter to homeowners hiring a roofer — storm damage experience, insurance claim assistance, manufacturer certifications, and warranty information. Roofing has a higher average ticket than most trades, and the site reflects that: the copy is more detailed, the trust elements are more prominent, and the CTA is oriented toward free estimates rather than emergency calls.

The services page covers residential roof replacement, roof repair, storm damage, gutters, and commercial flat roofing — a representative menu for a full-service roofing contractor. The city page demonstrates how local content is structured for a trade where service radius is particularly important for ranking.

  • Home, Services, About, Contact
  • 1 city service page
  • LocalBusiness + RoofingContractor schema
  • Storm damage and insurance claim section
  • Free estimate CTA structure

4 pages
Landscaping

GreenEdge Landscaping

Starter Plan demo

A four-page landscaping demo that addresses the specific content challenges of the landscaping vertical: seasonal service variation, the breadth of services offered (lawn maintenance, hardscaping, irrigation, cleanup), and the visual nature of the work. The services page is organized by category rather than a flat list, which makes it easier to build out individual service pages later.

Landscaping searches are often seasonal — spring cleanup, fall aeration, irrigation winterization — and the site copy reflects that without being locked into a single season. LocalBusiness and LandscapingBusiness schema is implemented with service area specification for local search targeting.

  • Home, Services, About, Contact
  • Seasonal service structure
  • LocalBusiness + LandscapingBusiness schema
  • Service category organization
  • Free estimate CTA
