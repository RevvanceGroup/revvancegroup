Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026. This page explains what information we collect when you use revvancegroup.com or work with us as a client, what we do with it, and how to contact us about it.

Who We Are

Revvance Group LLC ("Revvance Group," "we," "us") is a US-based web design and SEO agency. This privacy policy applies to revvancegroup.com and to any services we provide to clients.

What Information We Collect

Information you give us directly

When you fill out a contact form, book a discovery call, or email us, we receive the information you submit. This typically includes:

  • Your name and business name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Information about your trade, service area, and current website
  • Anything else you choose to tell us in the message field

Information collected automatically

Our website uses standard web technologies that collect basic information about your visit:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring URL (where you came from)
  • Approximate location based on IP address

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Plausible, or similar) to understand site usage in aggregate. We do not attempt to identify individual visitors from this data.

Calendly bookings

When you book a call through our Calendly link, Calendly collects your name, email, and any answers to questions on the booking form. Their privacy policy governs that data: calendly.com/privacy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or scheduling request
  • Build the free demo we offered you
  • Provide our website design and SEO services if you become a client
  • Send transactional messages (invoices, project updates, support replies)
  • Improve our website and our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your information. We do not share your information with third parties for their own marketing.

Marketing Email

If you fill out a contact form or book a call, we may send occasional follow-up emails about your project or about our services. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying with the word "unsubscribe" or by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send. We do not buy or rent email lists.

Cookies

Our website may use small text files (cookies) to support analytics and to remember basic preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not break the site.

How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information you give us. This includes encrypted connections (HTTPS), encrypted storage where appropriate, and limited internal access. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat your information the same way we'd want our own treated.

How Long We Keep Your Information

We keep contact form submissions and project communications for as long as we need them to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. If you ask us to delete your information and we are not under a legal obligation to retain it, we will do so within 30 days.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under privacy laws like the GDPR (EU/UK) or CCPA (California) including:

  • The right to know what information we have about you
  • The right to request a copy of your information
  • The right to ask us to correct or delete it
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@revvancegroup.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

Our services are intended for business owners and decision-makers. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, contact us and we will delete it.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to operate our business — for example, hosting providers, email service providers, analytics tools, and Calendly for scheduling. Each of these providers has its own privacy policy. We work with reputable providers and only share what they need to do their job.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where required by law, notify you directly. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email info@revvancegroup.com.
